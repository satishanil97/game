Mind Game

Player-1 : red

Player-2 : yellow

Player-1's turn

Player-1 starts the game with the color red, Player-2 is assigned the color yellow. Each player can take chances to click an empty hole to place their piece there.



Initially the game proceeds like tic-tac-toe, but after each player has placed their pieces in 3 holes, they cannot add any more. Instead they are supposed to move one of their existing pieces from its current hole to an adjacent unoccupied hole. Illegal moves are blocked automatically.



Inorder to move a piece, the player has to first click on it. This causes the piece to be colored green. To place the selected piece on a specific hole, click there. To deselect a chosen piece simply click on it again.



Now put your brains to work and get going :)
